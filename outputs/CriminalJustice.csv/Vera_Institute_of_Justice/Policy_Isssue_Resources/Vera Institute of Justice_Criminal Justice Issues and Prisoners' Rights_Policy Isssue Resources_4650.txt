People with mental illness do not fare well in correctional facilities, where they are more likely to be victimized and housed in solitary confinement. Historically, justice systems have been ill-equipped to address the needs of this population due to a lack of adequate treatment services coupled with poor collaboration with community-based health organizations.This briefing—with community and government leaders—examines how the Affordable Care Act and promising new initiatives may help abate this crisis.
