OrganizationsExplore ResourcesForging New Collaborations: A Guide for Rape Crisis, Domestic Violence, and Disability OrganizationsOverviewBetween 2006 and 2010, the U.S. Department of Justice, Office on Violence Against Women funded the development of collaborations between victim services and disability organizations in more than 40 communities to ensure people with disabilities who have experienced domestic or sexual violence have the community-based supports and criminal justice responses they need to heal.
