Mike DeWine freezes all Ohio executions while new method developedCOLUMBUS, Ohio — Gov. Mike DeWine said Tuesday that there will be no more executions in Ohio until a new method of carrying them out can be developed and deemed constitutional by the courts.“As long as the status quo remains, where we don’t have a protocol that has been found to be OK, we certainly cannot have any executions in Ohio,” DeWine told reporters at an Associated Press forum in Columbus. “That would not be right, at least in my opinion.”Read more here.Filed Under: News Copyright © 2022 · undesign on Genesis Framework · WordPress · Log in
