It is the absolute responsibility of government to protect the safety of inmates.Sexual abuse in detention, whether committed by staff or other people in custody, represents a government’s failure to uphold this responsibility. No matter what crime someone may have committed, rape is not part of the penalty.JDI is the only organization in the world dedicated exclusively to ending this kind of abuse.
