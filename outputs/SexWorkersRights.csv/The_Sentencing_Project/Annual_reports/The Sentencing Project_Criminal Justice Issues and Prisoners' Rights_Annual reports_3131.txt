Reforms that our work promoted in 2017 and that received wide coverage include:Investigating effective and humane ways of treating the opioid crisis without relying on failed drug war policies;Publishing reports highlighting the urgent need to address our growing lifer population in order to end mass incarcerationDocumenting that U.S. immigrants—regardless of legal status—commit
