Tom Smith with the Ohio Council of Churches is one of those urging Kasich to act on recommendations of an Ohio Supreme Court task force that studied the capital punishment process. Smith says there are many problems with the death penalty…including racial and economic disparities among those who are sentenced to death row and those who are given lighter sentences.
