The man who took his life, Thomas Knuff, was recently sentenced to death. Knuff took my dad from me and robbed the two of us of invaluable time together. It’s impossible to describe the depths of my pain or how to cope with what’s happened to me, but I know that Knuff’s death sentence will not provide healing to me.
