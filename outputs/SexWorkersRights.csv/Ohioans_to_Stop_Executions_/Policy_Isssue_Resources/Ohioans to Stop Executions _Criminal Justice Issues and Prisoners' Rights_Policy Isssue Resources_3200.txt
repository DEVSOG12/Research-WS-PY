Nevertheless, he recommended allowing Campbell to lie “in a semi-recumbent position” during the execution.The same exam failed to find veins suitable for inserting an IV on either of Campbell’s arms.The Department of Rehabilitation and Correction planned to move Campbell Tuesday from death row in Chillicothe to the death house at the Southern Ohio Correctional Facility in Lucasville, about 85 miles (137 kilometers) south of Columbus.He is set to die by injection Wednesday.Read more at abcnews.comFiled Under: News Copyright © 2022 · undesign on Genesis Framework · WordPress · Log in
