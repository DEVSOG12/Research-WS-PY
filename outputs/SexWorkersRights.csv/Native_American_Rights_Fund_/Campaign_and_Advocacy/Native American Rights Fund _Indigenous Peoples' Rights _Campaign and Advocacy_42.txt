NARF Staff Attorney Brett Lee Shelton (Oglala Lakota) was a contributing author to this timely and definitive paper exploring the issues that must be considered in order to protect the health of Native people.Impact of ACA Repeal on American Indians and Alaska NativesOVERVIEW OF KEY CONSIDERATIONS IN AMERICAN INDIAN HEALTH POLICYAs Congress begins debate on whether and how to repeal or replace the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act (ACA or “Obamacare”), American Indians and Alaska Natives (AI/ANs) face considerable risk and uncertainty due to many special provisions in the ACA that were intended to address longstanding underfunding and health disparities.
