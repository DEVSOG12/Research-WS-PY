The seven projects were The Court Employment Project (run by Vera), Operation de Novo, The Court Resource Program, the Hudson County Pretrial Intervention Program, the Bergen County Pretrial Intervention Program, the Dade County Pretrial Intervention Project, and Operation Midway.
