The files are arranged in the original order of the workbook, starting with the complete (374 page) workbook. The booklet contains the essay "Democratic Policing: a Framework for Action" by Chris Stone and Heather Ward. In addition, country overviews of South Africa, Uganda, Hungary, Poland, Russia, the United States, Brazil, Chile, Argentina and India are included.
