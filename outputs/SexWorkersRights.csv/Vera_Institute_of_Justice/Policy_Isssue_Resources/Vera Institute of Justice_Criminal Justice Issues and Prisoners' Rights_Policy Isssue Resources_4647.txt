A Review of State-level Drug Law Reform, 2009-2013Explore ResourcesDrug War Détente? - Full ReportDrug War Détente? - SummaryDrug War Détente? - InfographicOverviewSince 2009, more than 30 states have reformed existing drug laws and sentencing practices, passing nearly 50 bills in one or a combination of the following five areas: 1) mandatory penalties, 2) drug sentencing schemes, 3) early release mechanisms, 4) community-based sanctions, and 5) collateral consequences.
