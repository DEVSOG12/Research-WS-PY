Despite the challenges 2020 has given us all, with your help, Innocents at Risk has been able to continue to expand our awareness outreach trainings of Human Trafficking. Human Trafficking continues to be the fastest-growing criminal industry in the world, an industry of over $150 billion annually. It is the mission of Innocents at Risk to protect all women and children from this evil.Innocents at Risk has been working tirelessly through COVID-19 to alert parents that children have never been at a greater risk of online sexual exploitation. As children are spending more and more time on the Internet, traffickers have moved their operations online and they must be stopped. Innocents at Risk is dedicated to preventing online exploitation of children.Here are some of the 2020 Highlights of our Work.Going forward in 2021, Innocents at Risk will continue to work in partnerships with The Department of State, The Department of Homeland Security,The Department of Transportation, The Blue Campaign, the FBI, Operation Underground Railroad, The National Center of Missing and Exploited Children, NCOSE, Voices Against Trafficking, Airline Ambassadors, The Aviation Industry, the hotel industry, Truckers Against Trafficking, The D.C. Task Force Against Trafficking, The United Nations Blue Heart Campaign, American Mothers International, Teach for America, Enough is Enough, Christ Church Georgetown, Lincoln Park United Methodist Church, other places of worship, and many other organizations to raise awareness and educate communities, schools, and churches in the Washington D.C. area, the international and diplomatic community, the corporate sector, and the public to keep children and women safe everywhere.With your support throughout 2020, you have helped Innocents at Risk expand our mission to keep women and children safe on the internet and everywhere. Innocents at Risk looks forward to 2021 and the milestones we will make with your help.As this year comes to an end, please consider making a donation to Innocents at Risk so Innocents at Risk can continue to be a strong force in the fight against sexual exploitation. Please donate here or you may mail a check to the office of Innocents at Risk: 1101 30th Street NW, Suite 500, Washington, DC. 20007.Wishing you and your families a safe, healthy, happy holiday.Warmest Regards,Deborah SigmundFounder/Directorand the Innocents at Risk TeamNewer PostThe 2020 UNODC Global Report on Trafficking in Persons is NOW availableOlder PostTwo Teen Girls Present First-Class Tickets, Airline Attendant Notices They're In Real DangerChild trafficking is happening in every corner of the world, in major cities and small towns, and in every state and province.
