Kelly Sentenced to 30 Years in PrisonDeborah SigmundJuly 1, 2022Dear Friends of Innocents at Risk,On Wednesday, June 29th, R. Kelly was sentenced to 30 years in prison on sex trafficking charges. The prosecutors had accused Kelly of using his fame and influence to exploit and traffic young girls.Innocents at Risk is very grateful for the 30-year sentence that R. Kelly has received for his horrendous crimes. We are thankful that justice has been served for the survivors of Kelly’s atrocities. This sentencing goes to show that no one is above the law, no matter how famous or powerful you are.Please read more about the entire case in the article here.
