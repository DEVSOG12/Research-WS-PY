Incarcerated Young PeopleIn response to Dr. Amanda Klonsky’s opinion piece published in the New York Times today regarding the risk that COVID-19 poses in our nation’s jails and prisons, Sandy Santana, executive director of Children’s Rights, issued the following statement:“With schools closing and colleges sending students home, New York City must not ignore the health and well-being of youth on Rikers and in other juvenile facilities. As many of these young people who can be safely sent home must be released immediately.
