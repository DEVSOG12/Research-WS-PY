Campbell already was on parole for killing someone when he killed Charles Dials during a carjacking.Campbell has a galaxy of medical problems. He uses a walker. He is hitched to a colostomy bag. He has chronic obstructive pulmonary disorder, and he takes four breathing meds a day.
