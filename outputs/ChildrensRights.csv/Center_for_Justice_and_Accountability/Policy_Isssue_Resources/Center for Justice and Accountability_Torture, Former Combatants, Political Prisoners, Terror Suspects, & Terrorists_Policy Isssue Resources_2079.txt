CJA has played a lead role in identifying gaps in U.S. civil and criminal law that let the worst international human rights criminals slip through the cracks. Working with policymakers and civil society groups, CJA crafts and supports policies that ensure the effective prosecution of atrocity crimes, deny safe haven to their perpetrators, and protect and rehabilitate their victims.Prior CampaignsVictory!
