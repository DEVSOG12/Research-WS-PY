Coulter, executive director of the Center, prepared the following presentation at the University of Colorado Law School’s forum Free, Prior and Informed Consent: Pathways for a New Millenium, held October 31, 2013. Coulter states the “free, prior, informed consent approach to indigenous rights is backward, confusing, and in practice, it is a disservice to indigenous peoples.” Click here to read more.
