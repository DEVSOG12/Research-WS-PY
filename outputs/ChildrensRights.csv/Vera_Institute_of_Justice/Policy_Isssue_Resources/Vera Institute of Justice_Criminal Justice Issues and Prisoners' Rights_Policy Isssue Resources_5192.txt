Opinion Research Project Submitted to the Fund for the City of New YorkExplore ResourcesFinal Report of the Opinion Research Project Submitted to the Fund for the City of New YorkOverviewThis is the final report on the Opinion Research Project, which tested the feasibility of a communication and opinion research center to provide New York City officials with information on housing, crime, health, and education that would not otherwise be available.
