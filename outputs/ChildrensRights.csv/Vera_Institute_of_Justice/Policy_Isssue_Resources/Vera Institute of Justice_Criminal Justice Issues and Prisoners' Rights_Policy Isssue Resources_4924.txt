The daylong meeting was organized by the Vera Institute of Justice in cooperation with the Violence Against Women Grants Office and the Violence Against Women Office and was facilitated by Vera’s director, Christopher Stone. The meeting began in the morning with a round of introductions, followed by three broadly-framed conversations, and ended late that afternoon with a round of concluding remarks.
