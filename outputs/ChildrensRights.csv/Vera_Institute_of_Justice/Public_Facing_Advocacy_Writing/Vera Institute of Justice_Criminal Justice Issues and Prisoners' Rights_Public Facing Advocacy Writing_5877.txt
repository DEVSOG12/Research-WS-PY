NYT debate on how violence affects youthAnnie Salsich—Nov 13, 2012SHAREA November 4 New York Times article on the corrosive effects of gang violence among Chicago youth drew on an ongoing two-decade-long study of young people caught up in this violent culture.Today, Annie Salsich, director of Vera's Center on Youth Justice, continued the conversation about the critical problem in a Letter to the Editor amplifying the importance of developing effective responses to violence that take into account the pain it inflicts on both the victim and the perpetrator.
