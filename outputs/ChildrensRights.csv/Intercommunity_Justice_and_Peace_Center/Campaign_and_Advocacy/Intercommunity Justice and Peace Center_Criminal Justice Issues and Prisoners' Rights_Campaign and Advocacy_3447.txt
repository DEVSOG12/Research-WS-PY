The Cincinnati Interfaith Vigil to End the Death Penalty will be held outside, near Bellarmine Chapel at Xavier University, in the North Yard Stage. Parking is available in the Cintas Lot. Please wear a mask.The event is supported by the Archdiocese of Cincinnati’s Offices of Catholic Social Action and Respect Life, Bellarmine Chapel, the Dorothy Day Center for Faith and Justice at Xavier University, and the Intercommunity Justice and Peace Center.If you represent a faith community that would like to participate in this event, we’d welcome you to join us!
