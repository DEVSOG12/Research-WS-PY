We cannot resume the federal death penalty.“I have seen the death penalty up close. During my years as Ohio’s Republican attorney general, I oversaw 18 executions. Before that, I helped write the state’s death penalty law as a legislator. I’ve heard all the justifications — deterrence, support for victims, cost savings. And I know now they are all wrong, and those misguided arguments act as a smoke screen for the profound flaws that mark capital punishment.”– Jim Petro, former Ohio Attorney General and former OTSE Board Member.Read more here.Filed Under: News Copyright © 2022 · undesign on Genesis Framework · WordPress · Log in
