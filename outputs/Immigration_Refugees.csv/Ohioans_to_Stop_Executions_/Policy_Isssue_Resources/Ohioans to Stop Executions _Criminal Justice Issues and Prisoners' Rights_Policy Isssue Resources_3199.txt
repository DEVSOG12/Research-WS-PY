Nevertheless, he recommended allowing Campbell to lie “in a semi-recumbent position” during the execution.The same exam failed to find veins suitable for inserting an IV on either of Campbell’s arms.Campbell arrived at the death house at the Southern Ohio Correctional Facility at 9:59 a.m. Tuesday in Lucasville, about 85 miles (137 kilometers) south of Columbus.
